Peterhouse Christmas Recording

Dec 18, 2024  •  1 minute

Every year, Peterhouse Choir releases a recording of a carol for Christmas. For this year that I am in the choir, we sang “Eit barn er født i Betlehem”, a Norwegian traditional piece arranged by Ørjan Matre – part of a trend of the choir singing Scandinavian choral repertoire.
