About

Hello there 👋 ! Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I’m currently working as CAD Design Verification Engineer at Apple. I previously studied at the University of Cambridge for my MPhil, and at the University of Warwick for my BSc, both in Computer Science. I have spent some time as an academic officer at the University of Warwick Computing Society and as an intern at Apple.

In my free time I enjoy choral singing, always try to travel by bicycle instead of walking (irrespective of how short the journey is), and occasionally rock climb. When I have the time and opportunity, I also work on various side projects – some of which I finish!
